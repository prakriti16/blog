CBSE Board Exam Tips (Applicable for both 10th and 12th)

  1. Read NCERT atleast twice. Trust me, the second time is when you actually start absorbing stuff. I remember seniors saying they solved the math book three times too! So that was my aspiration but I never was able to find enough time to do it because I am slow at reading I guess?
  2. Pleas please please read through the toppers answer sheets from previous years. Not only do Board Exam questions repeat but also the answers written by these students are so so good you can literally just mug it up and paraphrase it during your exam. selfstudys.com is where I went to see these usually but you can explore any website you like.
